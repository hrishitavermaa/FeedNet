About Us

Welcome to FeedNet, where we tackle two of the world's pressing issues: food waste and hunger. Our innovative food waste management system not only minimizes waste but transforms it into a vital resource for those in need.

Partnering with over 150 banquets, we provide a robust platform for individuals and organizations to donate surplus food. This collected food is then carefully distributed to those facing food insecurity, ensuring that nutritious meals reach the people who need them most rather than ending up in landfills.

Join us in our mission to feed people, not landfills. Together, we can create a sustainable future where no one has to go hungry.
